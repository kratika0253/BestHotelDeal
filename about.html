About

History

Nurtured from the seed of a single great idea - to empower the traveller - Bharattrotter is a pioneer in India’s online Hotel Booking Industry. Founded in the year 2018 by Kratika and Tanya Motwani, Bharattrotter came to life to empower the Indian traveller with instant bookings and comprehensive choices.

The company initiated its journey serving the US-India travel market offering a range of best-value products and services powered by technology and round-the-clock customer support.

Bharattrotter consolidates its position in the market as a brand recognised for its reliability and transparency. With more and more Indians initiating to transact online with IRCTC and new opportunities with the advent of low cost carriers, Bharattrotter offered travellers the convenience of booking travel online with a few clicks

Our Company’s culture of continuous innovation and “customer first” strategic focus in India’s travel market has allowed us to be the clear leader in the industry and positions us to accelerate the predominately offline hotels and accommodations market online.

Bharattrotter's rise has been led by the vision and the spirit of each one of its employees, for whom no idea was too big and no problem too difficult. We aim to give the best possible user experience across their entire travel journey, which includes effective planning resources, superior booking experience across all channels and in journey 24x7 live customer support. With untiring determination, MakeMyTrip has proactively diversified its product offering, adding a variety of online and offline products and services.

Bharattrotter has stayed ahead of the curve by continually evolving its technology to meet the ever-changing demands of the rapidly developing global travel market, steadily establishing itself as India’s leading online travel company.
